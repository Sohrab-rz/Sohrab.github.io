About

Sohrab is a researcher specializing in the control of complex dynamical systems, with expertise in data-driven and AI methods for system analysis and control. He develops strategies to enhance the efficiency, resilience, and real-time performance of high-dimensional systems.

His work includes non-parametric control (NPC) methods, analysis of structural properties in multivariable systems, and research on data-driven control allocation for over-actuated systems.

He is particularly interested in data-driven approaches that bridge theory and practice, aiming to advance next-generation methodologies for modeling and control of complex systems.
